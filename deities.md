# Deities of Aern

## The Ancients
The Ancients are teh first beings of this universe. To understand them would take more than a mortal brain is capable of. To witness one would cause insanity

## The Estasi
The [Estasi](/deities/Inestasi) are the creators of Aern

## The Inestasi
The [Inestasi](/deities/Inestasi) are gods of Aern just like the [Estasi](/deities/estasi), except that they never took part in it's creation and don't care for it's existence.
