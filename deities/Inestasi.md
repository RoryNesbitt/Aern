# Inestasi

The Ancients built the universe. The [Estasi](/deities/estasi) built Aern. The Inestasi said "I'm going home and I'm taking my ball with me."

The Inestasi are deities, just as the Estasi are, born of the same ancient magic of [Inizio](/inizio). Where the Estasi chose to shape and shepherd the mortal world, the Inestasi turned away from it — retreating to [Inferno](/inizio#inferno), a realm of their own making, layered and vast. It is unknown how many Inestasi there are in total, but those known to have had some influence on Aern are recorded here.

Unlike the Estasi, the Inestasi are not widely worshipped. They are feared, bargained with, or simply avoided. Some have chosen to interact with Aernians on their own terms. Most have not.

---

## The Known Inestasi

### Sventura
*The Shrouded Queen*
**Realm:** [Caer Strazio](/inizio#caer-strazio) | **Influence:** Unknown | **Nature:** Sovereign

Sventura was the first Inestasi. Unwilling to share in the shaping of Aern, she created Inferno as a world entirely her own, then opened its deeper layers to the other Inestasi to claim and shape as they wished. She watches Aern from afar with detached curiosity — not hatred, not affection — and cares little for its people so long as they stay out of her palace, Caer Strazio.

---

### Ladro
*The Thief / The Lucky One*
**Realm:** [The Lost Hand](/inizio#the-lost-hand) | **Influence:** Theft, Luck | **Nature:** Trickster

Ladro is one of the few Inestasi who genuinely cares for Aernians — or at least finds them entertaining enough to look after. He is patron to the Thieves Guild and created [The Lost Hand](/inizio#the-lost-hand) as a means of interacting with them directly. Ladro is the only Inestasi known to show any interest in gold.

---

### Abisso Notte
*Abinot / The Great Architect*
**Realm:** [Labirinto](/inizio#labirinto) | **Influence:** Architecture, Invention | **Nature:** Architect

Abinot was the mind behind Sventura's grand palace. He created [Labirinto](/inizio#labirinto) as his masterwork — a testament to both his architectural skill and his mechanical ingenuity. Though he has no love for Aernians, he has on several occasions influenced their building designs purely for his own pride. When one of these structures became the foundation for drawing magic directly from the gods, Abinot's solution was characteristically detached: he suggested housing the mechanism inside a construct.

---

### Ateo Dannato
*Jarl of Nightfield*
**Realm:** Nightfield | **Influence:** Resilience, Community | **Nature:** Wanderer

Of all the Inestasi, Ateo's change of heart is perhaps the most surprising. Watching how the other Inestasi toyed with Aernians — and how stubbornly those Aernians refused to be broken — he developed a genuine respect for them. He eventually chose to walk among them, entering a small village and helping to build it into something worth keeping. He now holds the title of Jarl of Nightfield. Rumours of his strange powers circulate among the people, but as long as the village thrives, no one asks too many questions.

---

### Furia
*Ferocia / Fury / The Great Warrior*
**Realm:** [The Fields of War](/inizio#the-fields-of-war) | **Influence:** War, Rage | **Nature:** Warrior

Furia is the war deity of the Inestasi, though she fights for no cause other than the love of the battle itself. She has no interest in victory, territory, or glory in the way that [Vittoria](/deities/estasi#vittoria) or [Cavali](/deities/estasi#cavali) might understand it. What she respects is brutality — the unleashing of pure, unrestrained rage against an opponent. She does not distinguish between sides.

---

### Infausto
*Faust / The Knowledge Hoarder*
**Realm:** [The Dark Library](/inizio#the-dark-library) | **Influence:** Knowledge, Obsession | **Nature:** Collector

Faust is a vast, bloated creature — a mass of eyes and reaching appendages, drifting through his layer of Inferno, each eye fixed on a different piece of knowledge he does not yet fully possess. He collects all literature, storing it in his [Dark Library](/inizio#the-dark-library). Books created within it can become powerful artefacts if they find their way to Aern. His obsession is at its worst when it comes to the books of history kept in [Dati's](/deities/estasi#dati) study, which he has never been able to reach.

---

### Oscuro
*Secoro / Osco / The Assassin*
**Realm:** Unknown | **Influence:** Contract, Death | **Nature:** Assassin

Secoro sees himself as a service to Aern rather than a threat to it. He is patron to an assassins guild that operates under a strict ritual of contract. Every contract must name a target, state a price, and give a reason. If any of these are deemed inadequate, a guild representative may arrive to negotiate — but only they can cancel a contract, and only before the price has been formally agreed.

---

### Ammazzare
*Amma Zara / Mazze / The Spider Queen / Mother of Monsters*
**Realm:** [The Demonweb](/inizio#the-demonweb) | **Influence:** Monsters, Corruption | **Nature:** Corruptor

Mazze is human from the waist up and spider from the waist down, her lower half vast and many-legged. She is the only Inestasi capable of using living bodies to create demons, weaving them into Demonsilk cocoons within the [Demonweb](/inizio#the-demonweb). Her demons are uniquely monstrous, and she frequently sends them to drag victims down through the [Vile Caves](/vile-caves) and back to her. The lowest chasms of the Vile Caves connect directly to her layer of Inferno, and have been shaped by its influence over time.

---

### Rovina
*The Demon of Deals*
**Realm:** Unknown | **Influence:** Desperation, Souls | **Nature:** Broker

Rovina is the one who appears when people are at their most desperate. She is known to offer gifts in exchange for a person's soul, though what she intends to do with them remains unknown. She does not seek out victims — she simply arrives when the conditions are right, and waits.

---

### Maligno
*Rami Malig / Malo / Destroyer of Worlds*
**Realm:** [Nulla](/inizio#nulla) | **Influence:** Destruction, Disease | **Nature:** Destroyer

Malo holds the most hatred toward Aernians of any Inestasi — toward the planet itself, in fact. He is the reason fire spreads so quickly. He is the creator of many of the diseases that have plagued Aern's history. He eventually stopped devising new ways to kill when he realised he hated the dead just as much as the living. Instead, he created [Nulla](/inizio#nulla) — his layer of Inferno, a plane of absolute emptiness. It is the only place Malo has ever felt at peace.
