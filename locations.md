# Locations of Aern

The world of Aern is broadly divided into two landmasses — or was, before the Convergence. What were once two separate continents are now a single supercontinent, split down the middle by the vast mountain range thrown up during the collision.

## Pre-Convergence

Before the Convergence, Aern had two distinct continents separated by open sea.

The larger, western continent was home to the Empire — a civilisation built on trade, arcane technology, and the merchant parliament that governed it. Its capital was [Trademoor](/locations/pre-convergence/trademoor), and its most celebrated institution was [Ashspire](/locations/pre-convergence/ashspire), the great university town.

The smaller, eastern continent — [Ferrath](/locations/pre-convergence/ferrath) — was largely uncolonised, home to nomadic tribes and a handful of permanent camps. It was known for its unusual closeness to other planes of existence. It was here that [the Technomancer](/history/the-technomancer) chose to build his second tower.

## Post-Convergence

The Convergence merged the two continents into one, throwing up [the Ridge](/locations/post-convergence/the-ridge) at the point of collision. Civilisation survived on the western side, damaged but rebuilding. The eastern side — now the wild lands beyond the Ridge — remains dangerous and largely unsettled.
