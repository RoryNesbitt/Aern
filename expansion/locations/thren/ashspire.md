# Ashspire

The temple of [Dati](/deities/estasi#dati) stands at the centre of Ashspire, and has done for longer than most institutions on Thren have existed. It is the foremost place of learning in the empire — not because it claims to be, but because the knowledge accumulated within its walls, and the people who have passed through them, speak for themselves.

Ashspire is not a university that happens to be housed in a temple. It is a temple whose practice is the pursuit of knowledge in all its forms. To study here is to study under [Dati — the Scholar](/deities/estasi#dati). Wether you join as a researcher or as a worshipper, the work here is our devotion to the Lorekeeper.

## What Ashspire Offers

The temple's records represent centuries of accumulated knowledge across every field its priests have turned their attention to. Students at Ashspire work alongside researchers who have spent their lives in serious pursuit of their subject — from historians, naturalists, and arcane theory to the upcoming field of technomancy. The standard expected is high. The resources available to meet it are considerable.

Those who study here leave with something that cannot be replicated elsewhere on Thren — a depth of understanding shaped by an institution that has never been willing to treat knowledge as a means to a practical end. Knowledge, at Ashspire, is the end.

## The Layout

Seven towers rise above the temple grounds. They are the heart of Ashspire's most significant research, and assignment to a tower project is the highest distinction the temple can offer a student or researcher. Most who come to Ashspire will work in the main buildings. This is not a lesser experience — the main buildings are where the foundation is laid, and without the foundation the towers are meaningless.

Those who are ready for the towers will be found. The senior priest does not miss much.

## Admission

Ashspire accepts students on the basis of genuine aptitude and hunger for the work. Applications are considered by the temple's council of senior priests. Background, wealth, and prior connection to the temple carry less weight than the quality of a candidate's thinking and the seriousness of their intent.

Those who are accepted will find accommodation, a working market, and everything required to focus entirely on their studies. Those who require more than that from a place tend to find Ashspire is not the right fit.

## Getting Here

Ashspire is connected to Trademoor by lightning train. The journey is quick. You will spot the towers before anything else.
