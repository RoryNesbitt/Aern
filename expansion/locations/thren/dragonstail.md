# Dragonstail Reach

Dragonstail Reach sits on the south side of Thren, on the Dragonstail River. It is the empire's western military installation — a castle outpost built less out of necessity than precaution, positioned to defend a border that has never yet needed defending.

## Purpose

In the Age of Expansion, Dragonstail Reach is not a place most people think about. The empire is stable, trade is flourishing, and the threats the Reach was built to guard against have not materialised. It functions as a garrison, a training ground, and a quiet reminder that the empire has the capacity for defence even when defence is not required.

The soldiers stationed here are well trained and, by most measures, underused. The Reach produces capable military personnel who rotate out to other postings across the empire, and it maintains a readiness that the relative peace of the age makes easy to forget the purpose of.

## The Dragonstail River

The Dragonstail runs from the Stillmere south to the sea, passing alongside the Reach before reaching the coast. It is navigable for most of its length and used for the supply of the garrison, though it sees little other traffic.
