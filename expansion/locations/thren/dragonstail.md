# Dragonstail Reach

Dragonstail Reach sits on the western bank of the Dragonstail River, roughly two thirds of the way south from the Stillmere toward the coast. It is the empire's southernmost military installation — a castle outpost built less out of necessity than precaution, positioned to defend a continent that has not yet needed defending.

## Purpose

In the Age of Expansion, Dragonstail Reach is not a place most people think about. The empire is stable, trade is flourishing, and the threats the Reach was built to guard against have not materialised. It functions as a garrison, a training ground, and a quiet reminder that the empire has the capacity for defence even when defence is not required.

The soldiers stationed here are well trained and, by most measures, underused. The Reach produces capable military personnel who rotate out to other postings across the empire, maintaining a readiness that the relative peace of the age makes easy to forget the purpose of.

## The Dragonstail River

The Dragonstail runs from the Stillmere south to the sea. It is navigable for most of its length and used for the resupply of the garrison, though it sees little other traffic in peacetime.
