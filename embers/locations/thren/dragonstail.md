# Dragonstail Castle

Dragonstail Castle sits on the western bank of the Dragonstail River, roughly a third of the way south from the Stillmere toward the coast. It is the seat of House Reach — the ruling family of the southern kingdom — and the largest military power on Thren.

## The Kingdom

The Reach has been governed by the same lineage for as long as anyone in the south can remember. House Reach traces its authority to the King who held the southern lands together during the chaos of the Convergence, when supply lines failed and contact with the north collapsed. That King's leadership became the foundation of everything that followed, and their descendants have ruled here since.

The kingdom that grew around the Reach is stable, well defended, and economically independent. The Dragonstail River provides a trade route south to the coast. The surrounding settlements look to the Reach for governance and protection, and by most accounts receive both.

## The Claim

House Reach holds that governance requires more than a location. Legitimate rule, in their view, is demonstrated — through lineage, through sustained protection of the people, through the building of something that functions and endures. The merchants guild in [Trademoor](/embers/locations/thren/trademoor) offers a city and a tradition as argument. The Reach offers a kingdom and a proven line of rulers.

The contest between these two positions has shaped the politics of Thren throughout the Age of Embers. Neither side has been willing to force a conclusion. Neither has been willing to concede one.

## The Dragonstail River

The Dragonstail runs from the Stillmere south through the kingdom and out to the sea. It is the economic artery of the south, navigable along most of its length and used for trade between the Reach and the coastal settlements below it.
